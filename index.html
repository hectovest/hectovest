About Us

Hectovest was established in 2019 as a financial market observer community, focusing on stocks and other financial products. We are committed to continually focusing on investment education and deeper financial management.

Don't Lose Your Money

This video covers Warren Buffett's two main investment rules: "Don’t lose your money" and "Don’t forget rule number one." It reveals that losses from fraudulent investments in Indonesia reached Rp. 126 trillion (2008-2022) and highlights the low financial literacy in Southeast Asia and Africa

What To Do Before Investing

When is the right time to invest?" It advises ensuring you have emergency savings, health and life insurance, and a solid understanding of investment products. It also suggests choosing investments that match your profile, such as property, paper assets, or commodities.

Investing or Speculating

This video covers "4 Things to Know Before Buying a Stock." Beginner investors often only know the stock type and purchase price. It's crucial to have a diversified portfolio and understand the stock type, purchase price, target selling price, and the number of shares to buy.

A Proven Method More Than 50 Years

This video highlights Value Investing, a strategy proven for over 50 years. It involves buying undervalued stocks of strong companies. Warren Buffett's success with this method is well-known.

The Snowball Effect

This video shows how having 10 stocks, where 7 decrease by 15% and 3 increase by 15%, can result in a 393.7% profit over 20 years. This is due to Compound Interest, which amplifies investment returns through compound growth and long-term diversification.

Are You Ready For The Six Wave?

This video explains the Innovation Cycle and its impact on investment decisions. The cycle illustrates the pattern of technological and industrial evolution, from water power to artificial intelligence (AI). Is understanding the Innovation Cycle alone sufficient for making investment decisions?
